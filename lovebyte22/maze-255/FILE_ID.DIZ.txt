MAZE 255 - A joystick-driven maze exploration
game that is both tricky and addictive. Fully
immersive graphics and sound effects keep you
on your toes as you explore the corridors. 
Forget about memorising the layout, advanced
algorithms generate a brand new maze every
time you play. Can you complete the labyrinth
before your 166 seconds are up? Plug your joy
stick into port 2 and SYS 4096 to find out!
